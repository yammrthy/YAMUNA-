Program summary

Maintenance at this weight and height is roughly 2200–2500 kcal/day. This protocol runs a 700–950 kcal daily deficit — enough for consistent fat loss without surrendering muscle mass or stalling your metabolism by week six.

02

Daily protocol

Same seven blocks, every day. Sunday's meal 2 substitutes chicken for poriyal — everything else is identical.

Snack 1

Lemon water

Juice of 1 lemon in plain water. No honey, no sugar — ever.

Meal 1

Eggs + guava + cucumber & carrot

2 whole eggs + 1 white, boiled. 100g guava. 100g cucumber/carrot, raw.

Snack 2

Nuts & seeds — 25g total

Almonds, walnuts, white and black sesame. The cap is the point — nuts run 550–650 kcal/100g.

Meal 2

Rice + dal + poriyal (chicken on Sunday)

40g raw rice, 30g raw dal, 150–180g vegetable in max 5g oil. Sunday: 120–150g raw chicken instead.

Snack 3

Buttermilk

200ml from low-fat curd. Plain — no sugar, no store-bought salt mix.

Meal 3

Mixed legume bowl

40–45g dry: sprouts, kidney beans, black & white chana, red beans, peanuts. Plus onion, tomato, spices.

Activity

10,000 steps

Tracked on a phone or band. Not estimated, not rounded up from memory.

07

Compliance rules

Measured. Not Eyeballed.

Break any of these consistently and the deficit disappears without you noticing.

01

Weigh rice and dal raw, on a scale. Eyeballing 40g vs 80g turns a deficit into maintenance.

02

Oil capped at 1 tsp per dish. Roughly 10g total cooking oil per day, no more.

03

Zero added sugar or honey. Not in lemon water, not in buttermilk, nowhere.

04

Nuts stay at 25g. At 550–650 kcal/100g, an "extra handful" adds 150+ untracked kcal.

05

10,000 steps means tracked, not estimated. Use a device, not a feeling.

06

Weigh in twice weekly, same conditions. Ignore daily fluctuation entirely.

07

Never skip a meal to "save calories." It trains you to binge later — it doesn't save anything.

08

Judge the result on Day 45, not Day 7. Early drops are water. Stay the course either way.
